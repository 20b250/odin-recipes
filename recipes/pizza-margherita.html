Low effort pizza margherita

Improve (a little) your main single dad dinner!

Ingredients

  • Any supermarket pizza dough
  • Fried tomato
  • Mozzarella

Steps

  1. Shape the dough to your preference.
  2. Add plenty of fried tomato.
  3. Add plenty of mozarella.
  4. Cook in the oven until the dough reaches light golden.
