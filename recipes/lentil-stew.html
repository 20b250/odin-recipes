Spanish tortilla

Cook the greatest spanish recipe like your abuela!

Ingredients

  • Four potatos
  • One big onion
  • Three eggs
  • Salt and olive oil

Steps

  1. Cut the onion in thin pieces. Add plenty of oil to the pan and fry the onion over medium heat.
  2. Cut the potatoes in thin pieces and add to the pan when the onion reaches golden brown.
  3. Fry over medium heat until the potato is soft.
  4. Pour everything into another recipient, add the eggs and salt and stir.
  5. Add to the pan again and cook over low heat for 3-4 min. Turn the tortilla around and repeat.
