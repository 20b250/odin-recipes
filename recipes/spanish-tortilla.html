Lentil Stew

Cook the ultimate lentil stew for dummies!

Ingredients

  • 400g of lentils
  • One big potato
  • Two carrots
  • One big onion
  • One garlic clove
  • 4 tablespoons of crushed tomato
  • Half a green or red pepper
  • One teaspoon of paprika
  • Salt and black pepper
  • Olive oil

Steps

  1. Cook the lentils in a big pot using 1,6L of cold water.
  2. Add the onion, garlic salt and pepper to the pot
